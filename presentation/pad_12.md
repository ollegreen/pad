### You're all set

Here's everything at a glance:

**Cmd + N** — new pad
**Alt + ←/→** — navigate pads
**Alt + C** — centered mode
**Cmd + P** — presentation mode
**Cmd + ,** — settings
**Cmd + B** — bold
**Cmd + I** — italic
**Cmd + T** — insert todo

Press **Cmd + N** to create your first pad. Happy writing.
